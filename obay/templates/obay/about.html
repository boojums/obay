What it is

This is a prototype of a simple "silent auction" style fundraiser, 21st century style.

The concept

  • US team members come up with items to be auctioned and submit them to the site. They can be their own items/tasks or ones donated by friends and family
  • Anyone can create an account and bid on items. Non-orieteering friends, too! (Especially non-orienteering friends!)
  • Once the auction is over the winner of each item makes a donation to the US Senior Team equal or greater to their bid. The team member responsibel for the item makes sure it gets to the winner. Simple!

Team USA thanks you for your support!
